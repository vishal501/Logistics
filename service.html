Services

  1. Ajay TransLink is a next generation logistics service providers.

Transport of over dimensional cargo (ODC):

Transport of ODC is often considered risky by many logistics partners due to the government restrictions. But we at Ajay Translink with several years of experience have developed an extensive database of en-route obstacles and have conquered this area of transportation to ensure an error free transportation with safety guaranteed.

Light weight cargo (LCL):

Light weight cargo transportation has major implications for logistics due to competitive markets, customization and more. With a global operational presence, we offer a wide variety of transportation services to ensure every customer’s needs are satisfied.

Handling transportation of glass and delicate products:

Managing transportation of glass is a poses a great challenge given its delicate nature. Manufacturers and dealers can rely on us and our comprehensive network and equipments to ensure the cargo is transported safely.

Provide transportation of IT Products/ Electrical Appliances:

Transportation of electrical appliances requires specially-adapted equipment which are dry, adequately ventilated and can maintain appropriate temperature. We at Ajay Translink own all these resources and have proved to be reliable to all our partners.

Export- Import of Cosmetic Products:

The cosmetics industry is a rapid growing industry with sensitivities with product delivery lifecycle. With temperate control containers and all government permits, you can trust us to deliver your products on time with quality checks.

Logistics services for movement of Heavy Industrial Tools:

With years of experience, latest technology, labour and a team of highly skilled veterans, at Ajay Translinks, we ensure quality and professionalism in our service. 

Storage, loading& unloading of Hazardous Cargo like Petroleum Oil transport:

At Ajay Translink we have expertise in handling hazardous cargo and every precaution is taken by us to protect people and the marine environment. We have a strong commitment, strict protocols and fully compliant with the regulations in place to ensure that these cargoes are handled safely. We specialize in handling the storage, loading, unloading and transportation of hazardous cargo with a dedicated team and latest technology at hand.
