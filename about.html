About Us

Ajay TransLink is a next generation logistics service providers. Our team of experts deliver industry leading logistics solutions that enable manufacturers to link with dealers and vice- versa.

Located at Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust (JNPT), Mumbai we facilitate the transport of freight globally.

We at Ajay Translink use cutting edge technologies to produce unmatched levels of performance and get your products to their end destination faster. We specialise in various import-export related services from transportation of ODC cargo, Light weight cargo, handling glass/delicate products to IT/Electrical products to cosmetic products to hazardous cargo, warehouses and more!

Why Us

Our team at Ajay Translink is a perfect blend of industry expertise with trusted logistic leaders. With our own warehouses and logistics technologies we provide fast and trusted logistics services. Our customer’s success is our end goal and we strive to provide them with nothing less.

At Ajay Translink your freight is in the safest hands. We offer guaranteed quality assurance, accuracy as well as the best competitive pricing thus making one of the most sought-after logistics company.
